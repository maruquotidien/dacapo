# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 26, 2026**

Thank you for using Da Capo ("the App"). This Privacy Policy describes how the App handles your information.

## TL;DR

**Da Capo is a fully local application. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data.** All task data is stored exclusively on your device.

## 1. Information We Do NOT Collect

- Names, email addresses, phone numbers, postal addresses, or any other personally identifiable information
- The content of tasks you create, reminder settings, or completion history
- Device identifiers, advertising IDs, or IP addresses
- Usage analytics, behavioral data, or crash reports

The App **does not require account registration** and has no login mechanism of any kind.

## 2. Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device only and **is never transmitted to any server**:

- Tasks you create (titles, recurrence rules, reminder times, etc.)
- Task completion history
- App preferences (theme, notification toggles, etc.)
- Pro unlock status (a single boolean value)

When you uninstall the App, all of the above data is deleted along with it. The App does not provide cloud backup; please rely on the operating system's device backup mechanisms (iCloud Backup / Google One).

## 3. Third-Party Services

The App uses only the following essential platform services:

### 3.1 Apple App Store / Google Play In-App Purchases

When you purchase "Da Capo Pro," payment processing is handled by Apple or Google. We never have access to your payment information (credit card numbers, Apple ID, Google account, etc.). We only receive a boolean indicating whether the purchase succeeded, which is stored locally on your device.

See:
- [Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)
- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### 3.2 System Notifications

The App uses the operating system's local notification APIs (iOS / Android native) to remind you of tasks. Notification content is generated and displayed entirely on your device and **never passes through any server**. You may revoke the App's notification permission at any time through your system settings.

### 3.3 Timezone Detection

The App reads your device's current timezone setting in order to schedule reminders correctly. This information is used locally only and is not transmitted.

## 4. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update this page and revise the "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review it periodically.
